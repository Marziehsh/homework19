A Travel App for Busy Users. As a UX/UI designer, I crafted Dulili to simplify the travel experience with a unique 'pause and play' function. Dive into this case study to explore how I designed a user-friendly solution that streamlines the travel process for the modern traveler on the go.

Dulili case study

Discovery

Through in-depth user research, we identified the pain points of modern travelers and the need for a streamlined travel app solution. Understanding the importance of a hassle-free experience, we delved into the user's journey and pain points.

Ideation

With a focus on user-centric design, we brainstormed and conceptualized a unique 'pause and play' function that allows users to manage their travel plans seamlessly. The emphasis was on simplicity and intuitive design to ensure a delightful user experience.

Testing

We conducted extensive usability testing to validate our design choices and ensure that the app's features were easy to use and understand. Real user feedback played a crucial role in refining the app's functionality and enhancing its overall usability.

Learning

Throughout the development process, we learned the significance of empathy-driven design and the impact of user feedback on product success. The journey with Dulili reinforced the importance of continuous iteration and improvement based on user experiences.
